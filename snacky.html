Overview

A news app designed for Gen Z to stay informed through fast, interactive, and shareable content.
Social media habits have shifted how Gen Z consumes news — they prefer short, visual, and interactive formats over traditional articles.

Snacky delivers bite-sized updates, daily voting, and community-driven discussions to make staying informed more engaging and social.

What is problem?

News media is facing problems with technological changes, shifting habits of young consumers, and the rise of fake news.

Our Target Audience

The primary target audience is Generation Z (Gen-Z) especially those who prefer fast, interactive news formats over traditional media.

The App

That helps Gen Z explore trending topics through daily polls, comment threads, and personalized feeds.
The app provides headline summaries, lets users vote on the trustworthiness of news, and offers topic-based feeds.

It encourages open discussion through comment sections, and includes features like a reaction system and interest selection for personalization.
