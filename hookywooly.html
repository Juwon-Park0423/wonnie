Overview

An app that helps beginners enjoy crochet with fewer mistakes and more confidence.
Crochet and knitting have become popular ways to relieve stress and improve focus since COVID. However, beginners often struggle with unclear tutorials.

Hooky Wooly uses 3D visual guides to help users clearly see stitches and hand movements, making the learning process easier, more intuitive, and enjoyable.

What is problem?

Those new to crochet and knitting often struggle due to a lack of clear visual instruction. This can lead to repeated mistakes and a loss of motivation during the learning process.

Our Target Audience

The target audience is hobbyists in their 20s and 30s who are new to crochet and knitting, and are looking for a relaxing and creative way to spend their free time.

The App

That helps beginners learn crochet and knitting through clear, interactive 3D guidance.
The app offers step-by-step 3D tutorials with multi-angle views, a skill checker to recommend suitable patterns, and a progress tracker. It also provides posture tips for comfortable crafting and a Yarn Warehouse feature to suggest alternative yarns when needed.
