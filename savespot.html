Overview

An app that fosters positive awareness of crime prevention and personal safety.
Auckland is the largest city in New Zealand and home to many university students. However, the crime rate in Auckland City is rising.

This app allows students to review and share information about dangerous areas or incident locations through a map, helping them live more safely.

What is problem?

The crime rate in Auckland is continually increasing, and among its areas, Auckland City has the highest crime rate.

Our Target Audience

The primary target audience is university students based in the city, especially international students who may face additional safety challenges.

The App

That helps students stay safe by sharing and reviewing incident locations on a map.
